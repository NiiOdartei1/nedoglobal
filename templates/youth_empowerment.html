Empowering Ghana’s Youth

Driving innovation, entrepreneurship, and development through government-led initiatives

A National Commitment

The Government of Ghana is deeply invested in empowering its youth, creating opportunities that drive both individual and national growth. Through robust initiatives aimed at education, skill training, entrepreneurship, and employment, the government works to equip young people with the tools they need to shape a prosperous future.

Key Government Agencies & Programs

National Youth Authority (NYA)

Established to foster personal and professional development, the NYA plays a pivotal role in coordinating youth empowerment across Ghana, ensuring that young people have access to training, resources, and opportunities for creative expression and leadership.

Visit NYA
Youth Employment Agency (YEA)

The Youth Employment Agency is dedicated to reducing unemployment among Ghana's youth by facilitating skills development, start-up programs, and sustainable job creation initiatives. It serves as a bridge between vocational training and industry opportunities.

Explore YEA Initiatives

Recent Initiatives

Youth Enterprise Program

In partnership with private stakeholders, this program provides mentorship, seed funding, and business development resources to budding young entrepreneurs.

Tech Training & Innovation

Aimed at bridging the digital divide, this initiative ensures Ghana’s youth gain practical tech skills, preparing them for careers in the emerging digital economy.

Community Leadership & Engagement

This initiative encourages young leaders to participate in governance and community projects, fostering a spirit of volunteerism and local development.
