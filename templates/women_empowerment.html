Women Empowerment & Gender Equality

Transforming Lives Through Education, Entrepreneurship & Leadership

Overview

Empowering women is not only a moral imperative—it’s an engine for community transformation. By equipping women with skills, resources, and leadership opportunities, we foster self‐reliance and catalyze broader social change.

"When women are empowered, families thrive, communities prosper, and the world moves forward."

Programs & Plans

Our NGO has developed key programs that empower women through skill-building, financial independence, and mentorship.

Entrepreneurship & Vocational Training

Tailored programs in business management, digital literacy, and technical skills such as solar installation.

Microfinance & Savings Groups

Initiatives like The BOMA Project provide seed capital, mentorship, and financial training to support women-led businesses.

Community Mentorship

Programs inspired by the “Solar Mamas” of Barefoot College, guiding women to take on leadership roles in their families and communities.

Benefits of Women Empowerment

  • Increased household income and financial stability
  • Greater social respect and inclusion
  • Long-term poverty reduction and improved quality of life
