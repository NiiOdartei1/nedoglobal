About Us

Driving economic growth and sustainable development for communities worldwide.

Who We Are

National Economic Development Organisation (NEDO) is a non-governmental organization committed to promoting sustainable development, economic empowerment, and social transformation. Through education, entrepreneurship, and infrastructure projects, we equip communities with the resources and skills they need to thrive.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities by fostering economic growth, promoting innovation, and ensuring sustainable livelihoods.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has the opportunity to contribute to and benefit from sustainable economic development.

Our Values

  • Integrity & Transparency
  • Community-Centered Development
  • Innovation & Sustainability
  • Empowerment & Inclusivity

What We Do

Our initiatives are designed to create lasting impact and sustainable solutions.

Education & Skills Development

Providing access to education and vocational training for a skilled workforce.

Agriculture & Food Security

Promoting modern farming techniques to ensure sustainable food production.

Green Energy & Sustainability

Advancing renewable energy solutions to combat climate change.
